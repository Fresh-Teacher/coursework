Welcome to Justice League Sidekick Recruitment Program

This initiative was created by the students of Victoria University in Group 16.

Our mission is simple: give everyone and anyone a chance to serve by a great hero's side!

This project was made possible by the incredible creativity, dedication, and skills of our team members. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to Kristen Saad, our group leader, and Fresh Teacher, the head of construction, for their outstanding leadership and guidance throughout the project.

Special thanks to Moi⁩ and the rest of the team for providing the captivating images that brought our vision to life. Each member contributed their unique talents, time, and effort, making this initiative a reality.

Together, we've built a platform that not only inspires but also empowers individuals to embrace their inner hero. We couldn't have done it without the passion and commitment of every team member.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals to embrace their unique abilities and use them for the greater good. By connecting aspiring sidekicks with experienced mentors from the Justice League, we aim to foster a community of heroes dedicated to protecting the world from evil forces.
