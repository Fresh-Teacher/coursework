Game Controls

Controls:

  • UP ARROW: Uppercut
  • DOWN ARROW: Headshot
  • LEFT ARROW: Block
  • RIGHT ARROW: Punch
  • LEFT, RIGHT: Triple Punch

About the Game

In this action-packed game, you step into the shoes of the iconic superhero Spider-Man as he faces off against his arch-nemesis Venom in an epic battle to save the city. As Spider-Man, you must harness your agility, reflexes, and combat skills to defeat Venom and thwart his evil plans.

With the lives of the city's inhabitants hanging in the balance, you must navigate through dynamic environments filled with obstacles and challenges while engaging in intense combat sequences against Venom. Each punch, kick, and web-slinging maneuver brings you closer to victory as you strive to protect the city from imminent destruction.

As the fate of the city hangs in the balance, you must rise to the occasion, channelling the spirit of Spider-Man to overcome obstacles, outwit Venom, and emerge victorious. The stakes are high, and the responsibility falls squarely on your shoulders to ensure the safety and well-being of the city's inhabitants.
